# Lead to Cash

Lead to cash is the journey from a new customer opportunity to received payment. In IOTA SDK, this usually touches CRM, Projects, Warehouse, Finance, and Billing.

## Typical flow

1. A lead or customer is recorded in CRM.
2. The team communicates with the customer and captures context.
3. If work is required, a project or order is created.
4. Products, stock, or delivery details are checked in Warehouse when relevant.
5. Payments, expenses, debts, or transactions are recorded in Finance.
6. Online payment status may be handled through Billing.
7. Reports show the financial result.

## What each module contributes

- CRM keeps customer identity and communication history.
- Projects tracks work that must be delivered.
- Warehouse confirms product and stock availability.
- Finance records money movement and obligations.
- Billing handles provider-backed payment events.

## Business checks

- Is the customer record complete?
- Is there a clear owner for the work?
- Is stock available before promising delivery?
- Is the payment category correct?
- Is any debt still open after payment?

## Best practices

- Keep customer and counterparty records aligned.
- Record payment details as soon as money movement is confirmed.
- Review open debts before closing the customer case.
- Use consistent names across CRM, Finance, and Projects.
