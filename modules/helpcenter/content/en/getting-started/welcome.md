# Welcome to IOTA SDK

IOTA SDK is an ERP workspace for running the daily operations of a growing organization. It brings customers, sales activity, finance, inventory, people, projects, websites, and automation into one connected system.

The Help Center explains the business purpose of each module and how teams usually work with it. It is written for operators, managers, accountants, sales teams, warehouse teams, HR teams, and administrators.

## How to use this Help Center

- Start with the module pages to understand what each area owns.
- Use workflow pages when a process crosses several modules.
- Search for the business object you care about, such as client, invoice, stock, employee, project, subscription, or AI assistant.

## Main areas

- Core Administration: users, roles, permissions, company settings, and security.
- CRM: clients, conversations, templates, and customer-facing work.
- Finance: payments, expenses, accounts, debts, counterparties, and reports.
- Warehouse: products, inventory, units, positions, and orders.
- HRM: employees and positions.
- Projects: internal or client project tracking.
- Website: public website and AI chat experiences.
- BI Chat: AI-assisted business analysis and operational help.
- Billing: online payment providers and subscription payments.
- OIDC: single sign-on and identity integration.
- Super Admin: platform-level tenant and usage oversight.

## Good operating habits

- Keep customer, employee, product, and finance records clean.
- Assign permissions by responsibility, not by convenience.
- Review reports on a regular cadence.
- Use search before creating duplicate records.
- Treat modules as connected parts of the same operating picture.
