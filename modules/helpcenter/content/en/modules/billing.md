# Billing

Billing connects the ERP to online payment providers and subscription payment flows. It helps the business receive payments and process provider callbacks in a controlled way.

## Who uses it

- Finance teams
- Subscription managers
- Operations teams
- Platform administrators

## What it manages

- Payment transactions
- Provider callbacks
- Payment provider integrations such as Stripe, Payme, Click, and Octo
- Billing-related status updates

## Why it matters

Online payments are part of the customer experience and the financial record. Billing helps keep payment status, provider events, and internal records aligned.

## Common tasks

- Review payment transaction status.
- Confirm whether a provider callback was received.
- Investigate failed or pending payments.
- Coordinate subscription or payment issues with finance.

## Best practices

- Treat provider dashboards and ERP records as two sides of the same payment story.
- Investigate repeated failed payments promptly.
- Keep provider credentials and callback URLs controlled by trusted administrators.
- Reconcile billing records with finance reports.
