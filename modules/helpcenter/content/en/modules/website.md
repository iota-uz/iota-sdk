# Website

Website supports public-facing content and AI chat experiences connected to the organization. It helps teams manage the digital front door of the business.

## Who uses it

- Marketing teams
- Customer support teams
- Website administrators
- Product or operations teams that manage public information

## What it manages

- Website chat configuration
- AI chat experiences
- Public interaction flows
- Website-related content and cache behavior

## Why it matters

Customers often meet a business through the website before they speak to a person. The Website module helps keep that first interaction useful, consistent, and connected to the rest of the ERP.

## Common tasks

- Configure an AI chat experience.
- Review website chat behavior.
- Update public-facing guidance.
- Check that website interactions match current business rules.

## Best practices

- Keep public answers aligned with current policies.
- Review AI chat prompts and responses regularly.
- Coordinate website changes with CRM and support teams.
- Keep sensitive internal information out of public flows.
