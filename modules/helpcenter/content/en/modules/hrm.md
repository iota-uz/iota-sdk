# HRM

HRM helps the organization maintain employee and position records. It gives teams a simple, shared place for the people structure of the company.

## Who uses it

- HR managers
- Operations managers
- Department leads
- Administrators

## What it manages

- Employees
- Positions
- Employee-related organization data

## Why it matters

Clear employee records support access control, reporting, approvals, and accountability. HRM helps the ERP reflect the current team structure instead of relying on outdated spreadsheets.

## Common tasks

- Add a new employee.
- Update an employee's position.
- Maintain position names and responsibilities.
- Review team composition.
- Keep employee information current when roles change.

## Best practices

- Update HRM before changing system access.
- Keep position names consistent with the organization chart.
- Review inactive employee records regularly.
- Coordinate HRM updates with Core Administration roles and permissions.
