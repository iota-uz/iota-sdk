# Help Center

Help Center provides in-app documentation for the ERP. It gives users a searchable place to learn what modules do, how workflows connect, and where to find business guidance.

## Who uses it

- New users
- Support teams
- Department managers
- Administrators
- Anyone who needs quick guidance inside the ERP

## What it manages

- Markdown documentation pages
- Category navigation
- Searchable help content
- Localized content when translations are available

## Why it matters

Documentation is most useful when it is close to the work. Help Center keeps guidance inside the application so users do not need to leave the ERP to understand a module or workflow.

## Common tasks

- Search for a module, term, or business object.
- Open a category from the left navigation.
- Read workflow guidance before completing a process.
- Share a direct link to a help article.

## Best practices

- Keep articles short and focused.
- Write for business users first.
- Update docs when a process changes.
- Use consistent names for modules and business objects.
