# BI Chat

BI Chat is an AI-assisted workspace for asking business questions, exploring operational data, and getting help with analysis. It is designed to support decision-making without requiring every user to write queries or build reports manually.

## Who uses it

- Business owners
- Analysts
- Managers
- Operations teams
- Finance and sales leaders

## What it helps with

- Asking questions about business data
- Summarizing operational context
- Exploring reports and trends
- Working with uploaded files
- Capturing reusable learnings
- Using knowledge-base search

## Why it matters

Many business questions start in plain language: "Which customers are overdue?", "Why did expenses increase?", or "What changed this week?" BI Chat helps teams move from question to insight faster.

## Common tasks

- Ask for a summary of a business area.
- Upload a file for review.
- Search the knowledge base.
- Compare performance over time.
- Capture a useful answer for later reuse.

## Best practices

- Ask specific questions with a time period or business area.
- Review important answers before acting on them.
- Keep reusable business definitions documented.
- Do not paste secrets or sensitive personal data unless the workspace policy allows it.
