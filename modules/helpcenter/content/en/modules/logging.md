# Logging

Logging records important system events so administrators and operators can understand what happened in the platform. It supports monitoring, troubleshooting, and accountability.

## Who uses it

- Administrators
- Support teams
- Platform operators
- Security and compliance teams

## What it manages

- Operational logs
- System events
- Audit-friendly activity records

## Why it matters

When something unexpected happens, teams need a reliable way to trace activity. Logging helps answer questions such as when an event happened, which area was involved, and what should be investigated next.

## Common tasks

- Review recent events after an issue.
- Support an investigation.
- Monitor recurring problems.
- Provide operational context to administrators.

## Best practices

- Review logs as part of incident response.
- Keep access to logs limited to appropriate roles.
- Use logs together with business records instead of treating them as the full story.
